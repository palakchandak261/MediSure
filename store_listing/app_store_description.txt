MediSure — AI Prescription Manager

Scan any prescription with your camera and instantly get a structured list of medicines, dosages, and timing — powered by AI OCR technology.

WHAT MAKES MEDISURE DIFFERENT
MediSure works completely offline on your phone. No internet needed to scan prescriptions. Your health data stays private on your device.

SCAN PRESCRIPTIONS IN SECONDS
Point your camera at any prescription — handwritten or printed — and MediSure reads it for you. Supports English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam prescriptions.

SMART MEDICINE REMINDERS
Never miss a dose again. Set reminders for each medicine and get notified at the right time, even when the app is closed. Track your adherence with a 30-day calendar view.

FIND & ORDER MEDICINES
Locate pharmacies near you, compare prices, and order medicines with home delivery. Pay with UPI, cards, or cash on delivery.

COMPLETE HEALTH TRACKING
• Blood pressure, sugar, weight, temperature logs
• Drug interaction checker
• Medicine expiry date alerts
• Barcode scanner for medicine info
• Family health profiles

PRIVACY & SECURITY
Your health data is encrypted and stored locally. We never sell your data. Secure login with biometric support.
