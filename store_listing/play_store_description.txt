MediSure — AI Prescription Manager & Medicine Reminder

📋 SCAN PRESCRIPTIONS WITH AI
Take a photo of any prescription and MediSure instantly extracts all medicine names, dosages, and timing instructions using advanced OCR technology. Works offline on Android — no internet needed for scanning.

Supports prescriptions in 7 Indian languages: English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam.

💊 NEVER MISS A DOSE
Set smart medicine reminders with custom schedules. Get notified at the exact time you need to take your medicine — even when the app is closed. Snooze reminders, track missed doses, and view your 30-day adherence calendar.

🏪 FIND NEARBY PHARMACIES
Locate pharmacies near you using GPS. Compare medicine prices across Apollo Pharmacy, MedPlus, 1mg, and local stores. Order medicines directly from the app with home delivery.
